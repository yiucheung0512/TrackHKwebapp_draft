How the App Works

/01
Easy Input

Simply input the item and quantity consumed in each category and click on "add row" to input more items. Then, click "calculate" and your carbon emission report will be generated. (WORK BEST ON TABLETS AND COMPUTERS)

/02
Rich Infographics and Report

After recording your data in the app, press "calculate" to generate a report of your daily carbon output which help users identify major carbon making sources in their lifestyle and be aware of their environmental impact.
