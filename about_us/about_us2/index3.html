Our App

As programming enthusiasts, we made a webapp that estimates the daily carbon emission of the user. By learning more about your own habits, you will start to learn about what food you consume or what activities you participate in create the most carbon emission. Our webapp tracks your carbon emission from food consumption, transportation and utilities like water and electricity.
